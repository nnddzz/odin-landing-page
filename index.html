Our Features

Witness a Fiery Sunrise

Imagine yourself atop Mount Penanjakan, watching the first rays of dawn illuminate the peaks of Bromo, Batok, and Semeru.

Descend into the surreal panorama of the Tengger caldera, a vast expanse of volcanic sand known as the "Sea of Sand."

Feel the power of nature beneath your feet as you ascend the slopes of Mount Bromo also reach the crater rim and peer.
